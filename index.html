Building Your Bridge: The Power of the Soft Sell

For many professionals, the traditional job hunt is changing. Instead of applying to cold listings, many successful people now leverage the power of "soft sells." For example, they might initiate an informational interview to learn about a company's culture. This proactive approach helps you build a "bridge of trust" before a job even opens.

To succeed, you must be proficient in explaining your unique background. Even if you specialize in a specific technical field, you must adapt your story for a new audience. During these meetings, focus on being results-oriented. Talk about systems you developed or projects you helped facilitate in the past. This strategy moves the focus away from a high-pressure "ask" and toward a professional partnership. By the time a position is available, you have already resolved the employer's biggest fear: hiring a stranger.
